About US

We are a registered, secular, non-political, impartial, and incorporated non-profit organization for the Keralite community in Belgium. The main objective of this organization is to promote the socio-cultural integration of all Keralites living in Belgium. We organise social activities and cultural events frequently to connect and engage with each other. We believe it will also safeguard our legacy, culture, morals, language, and quality of unity even while staying away from our state.
